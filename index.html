    What is Western Dev Society?

    Western Dev Society is a club for:

    • aspiring developers
    • people interested in technology - specifically software development
    • students in technical majors and non-technical majors
      •

    Western Dev Society hopes to create a community for aspiring developers through:

    • our pro-bono development portfolio
    • educationals & workshops
    • competitions
    • socials
    • and conferences!
